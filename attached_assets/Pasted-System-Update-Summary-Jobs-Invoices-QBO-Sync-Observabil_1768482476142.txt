SLA tracking (24h warning, 72h escalation)

Escalation actions with audit events

Inline UI actions for notes & follow-up dates

Guarantees

Every status change produces a matching audit event

Technicians can update progress statuses only

Only office roles can close/reopen/escalate jobs
